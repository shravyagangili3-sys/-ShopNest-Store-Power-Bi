### ShopNest Store Power Bi
ShopNest Dashboard – Analytical Report
1. Overview

The ShopNest Dashboard provides a comprehensive snapshot of the company’s sales performance, revenue trends, customer order behavior, product category contributions, regional performance, and payment/ratings insights. The dashboard aggregates key metrics to support data-driven decisions across operations, sales, and customer satisfaction.

2. Key Metrics Summary

- Net Revenue: $163.1M

- Total Sales: $684.2M

- Total Orders (Delayed + On-time): 53.8M

- Delivery Status Filter: Allows segmentation by delivery performance.

- These figures highlight strong financial performance with substantial sales volume.

3. Sales and Revenue Insights
3.1 Revenue Trend Analysis (2017–2018)

The revenue chart demonstrates a significant upward trend, with revenue rising from roughly $4B in 2017 to approximately $6B in 2018.
This indicates:

- Strong year-over-year growth

- Improved customer acquisition or higher average order value

- Effective sales and marketing strategies

3.2 Top Categories by Total Price

- The highest-performing categories include:

- Beleza (Beauty)

- Relogios (Watches)

- Camping Gear

- Pet Supplies

- Informatica (Computing)

These categories contribute most to total revenue, suggesting strong consumer demand and effective product positioning within these segments.

4. Order Behavior Analysis
4.1 Monthly Comparison of Delayed vs. On-time Orders

- Across all months, on-time orders noticeably exceed delayed orders, reflecting strong supply-chain efficiency.

- Months such as March, April, June, and August show especially higher order volumes.

- The seasonal upward trends in certain months may reflect promotional periods or increased consumer activity.

4.2 Delayed Orders Analysis by Category

Categories with notable delayed orders include:

- Automotive

- Computing & Electronics

- Home Appliances

- Operational inefficiencies may be linked to heavier or high-value items, longer sourcing times, or logistics complexity.

5. Regional (State-Wise) Sales Performance

Top performing states include:

- TO, SP, SC, SE, RS, and RJ

These states account for the largest share of sales, indicating:

- Strong customer base concentration

- High product demand in urban/economic hubs

- Opportunities to expand marketing and logistics support in lower-performing regions

6. Seasonal Sales Patterns
 Quarterly sales distribution shows:

- Q2 as the highest-performing quarter

- Q3 following closely

- Q1 and Q4 showing lower but steady sales

This suggests stronger consumer spending during mid-year periods, possibly due to:

- Sales campaigns

- Holiday events

- Seasonal buying patterns

7. Payment Method Distribution

Payment methods appear heavily skewed toward:

- Credit card payments (dominant share)

- Other methods such as boleto, voucher, and debit card occupy smaller proportions

This indicates:

- Customer preference for convenience and flexibility of credit payments

- Strong alignment with online retail standards

8. Product Rating Analysis

- Ratings span across many product IDs.

- Majority of products receive ratings clustered around mid-to-high ranges.

- This suggests generally positive customer satisfaction across the catalog.

- Creating category-level rating segmentation would strengthen product quality insights.

9. Key Insights & Recommendations
9.1 Insights

- Revenue shows consistent upward growth.

- Top product categories are driving a significant portion of revenue.

- On-time delivery performance is strong but can be improved further in complex categories.

- Certain states and specific quarters outperform others.

- Credit cards dominate as the preferred payment method.

- Product ratings reflect overall positive customer experiences.

9.2 Recommendations

- Improve Logistics for High-Delay Categories
- Focus on supply-chain efficiency and partner performance for electronics, automotive, and large appliances.

- Expand Marketing in Underperforming States
- States with lower sales could benefit from targeted campaigns or improved delivery infrastructure.

- Strengthen Seasonal Promotions
- Enhance campaigns during low-performing quarters such as Q4.

- Diversify Payment Options
- Promote alternative payment methods (debit, wallet-based payments) to reduce reliance on credit card usage.

- Deepen Product Quality Analysis
- Drill down on low-rated SKUs to refine quality control and vendor management.
